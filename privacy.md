# Privacy Policy — NeuralPulse

**Last updated: March 2026**

NeuralPulse ("the app") is an automated video publishing tool that posts 
AI-generated tech news content to TikTok on behalf of the account owner.

**Data collection:** NeuralPulse does not collect, store, or share any 
personal data from TikTok users. The app only accesses the TikTok account 
of its owner to publish video content.

**Third-party services:** Videos are generated using AI services 
(Google Gemini, ElevenLabs) and published via the TikTok Content Posting API.
