Privacy Policy

App: NeuralPulse  |  Last updated: March 2026

NeuralPulse is an automated video publishing tool that posts AI-generated tech news content to TikTok on behalf of the account owner only.

Data Collection

NeuralPulse does not collect, store, or share any personal data from TikTok users. The app only accesses the TikTok account of its owner to publish video content.

Third-Party Services

Videos are generated using Google Gemini and ElevenLabs APIs, then published via the TikTok Content Posting API. No user data is passed to these services.
